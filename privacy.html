1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At AppBelieve Studio, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications and related services published under the AppBelieve Studio developer account on Google Play. Transparency builds trust — we want you to clearly understand what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Our apps may include features powered by trusted third-party services such as Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase (GA4), Google AdMob, and Google Ads, which help us improve performance, deliver personalized experiences, and support the continued development of free apps.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, or phone number) unless explicitly provided by you for specific purposes (e.g., contacting support). All analytics and advertising data are processed in compliance with applicable privacy regulations, including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional laws. Protecting your privacy is a core part of how we design and maintain our products.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect limited types of information to ensure our apps work properly, display relevant ads, and help us understand how users interact with our products. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable or sensitive data such as passwords, precise location, contacts, or financial information.

2.1. Data Collected Automatically

  • Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Anonymous identifiers (Advertising ID, Instance ID, or other non-personal identifiers)
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data
  • General location (country or region level only — not GPS)
  • Interaction data (e.g., app screens viewed, session duration)
  • Temporary cached images used for wallpaper display, never uploaded or shared externally
  • Media Access (Photos, Videos, Audio): access only local files for wallpaper display, never uploaded or transmitted

2.2. Data You Provide Voluntarily

If you contact us (e.g., via email or feedback form), we may collect the information you provide:

  • Name (if included)
  • Email address
  • Message content

We use this data only for responding to your inquiry or providing technical support.

2.3. Third-Party SDKs and Services

Our apps may use third-party SDKs that process limited technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (analytics, crash reporting): https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google AdMob & Google Ads: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. How Personal Data is Used

  • To Operate and Improve the App: Analyze app usage, fix bugs, measure engagement (Firebase, GA4, Crashlytics)
  • To Display Ads and Support Monetization: Serve ads via Google AdMob/Ads, personalized/non-personalized based on consent
  • To Communicate with Users: Respond to feedback, bug reports, support requests
  • To Ensure Compliance and Security: Detect/prevent fraud, maintain compliance with laws and policies

4. How Personal Data is Shared

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. Data is shared only with trusted third-party service providers or for legal reasons.

4.1. With Service Providers

  • Firebase – analytics, crash reporting, app performance
  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) – user interaction and event tracking
  • Google AdMob / Google Ads – ad serving, fraud prevention, campaign measurement

4.2. For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information to comply with laws, protect rights/property, or prevent fraud/security issues.

4.3. Aggregate and Anonymous Data

We may share anonymized data for research and analytics to improve app quality without affecting individual privacy.

5. How Personal Data is Retained and Secured

  • Data Retention: No personal data stored on our servers. Analytics, crash logs, ads metrics handled by Google.
  • Data Security: Trusted Google infrastructure, encryption, secure transmission. No direct access to raw identifiers.
