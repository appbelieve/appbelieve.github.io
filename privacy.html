1. Our commitment to Privacy

At AppBelieve Studio, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications and related services published under the AppBelieve Studio developer account on Google Play.

We believe transparency builds trust — that’s why we want you to clearly understand what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Our apps may include features powered by trusted third-party services such as Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase (GA4), Google AdMob, and Google Ads, which help us improve performance, deliver personalized experiences, and support the continued development of free apps.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, or phone number) unless explicitly provided by you for specific purposes (e.g., contacting support). All analytics and advertising data are processed in compliance with the GDPR, CCPA, and other regional laws.

Protecting your privacy is a core part of how we design and maintain our products.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect limited types of information to ensure our apps work properly, display relevant ads, and help us understand how users interact with our products. We do not collect or store any personally identifiable or sensitive data such as passwords, precise location, contacts, or financial information.

This information helps us:

7. App Permissions

Our app may request certain device permissions to enable its core features and support advertising or analytics services. These permissions are used only for their intended technical purposes. We do not use them to collect personal information or access your private content.

Specifically, the app may request:

  • Internet and Network Access (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): required to load online content, display ads, and send anonymous analytics data through Google services.
  • Advertising Identifiers (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID, ACCESS_ADSERVICES_AD_ID, ACCESS_ADSERVICES_ATTRIBUTION, ACCESS_ADSERVICES_TOPICS): used by Google Ads and AdMob to deliver and measure personalized or contextual advertising.
  • Billing Permission (com.android.vending.BILLING): enables secure in-app purchases or subscriptions via Google Play.
  • Wallpaper Access (SET_WALLPAPER): allows the app to set images as device wallpapers at the user’s request.
  • Storage Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): allows reading and saving wallpaper images for display only. The app does not access personal photos or files.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): optional permission that lets the app send notifications such as updates or reminders, only if the user grants consent.
  • Foreground and Wake Permissions (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, WAKE_LOCK): ensure that wallpaper or background processes can run smoothly without interruption.
  • Install Referrer Service (BIND_GET_INSTALL_REFERRER_SERVICE): used by Google Play to measure installs and app performance metrics.

Storage and Media Access Clarification: When granted, the storage permission (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) may technically allow access to media files such as photos or videos stored on your device. However, our app only reads image files selected or displayed as wallpapers and does not access, scan, upload, or store any other personal media content such as your personal photos, videos, or audio recordings. No media data is ever transmitted off your device. All access remains local and limited strictly to the wallpaper functionality requested by the user.

AppBelieve Studio does not access, store, or share any personal data, files, photos, or location information from your device.

  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal data provided by or obtained about you.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a portable and readily usable format, where technically feasible.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt out of the processing of your personal data for targeted advertising, the sale of personal data, or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at dev.appbelieve@gmail.com with the subject line “Virginia Privacy Request.” We may request verification of your identity before processing your request. If you disagree with our response, you may appeal by replying to our decision email.

10. European Union Privacy Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent laws. We act as a data controller for personal data we collect directly and as a data processor for data handled through third-party services (such as Firebase, GA4, AdMob, and Google Ads).

Your privacy rights include:

  • Right of Access: Request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and obtain a copy.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct or update inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion when no longer necessary or when you withdraw consent.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit processing of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to our processing of your personal data for direct marketing or other purposes based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing based on consent (e.g., analytics or personalized ads) at any time.

To exercise these rights, email dev.appbelieve@gmail.com with the subject line “GDPR Privacy Request.” We will respond in accordance with applicable EU data protection laws. You also have the right to file a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

11. Brazil’s Privacy Rights

If you are located in Brazil, you have specific rights under the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD) regarding your personal data.

Your rights under LGPD include:

  • Right to Confirmation and Access: Ask whether we process your personal data and request access.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated information.
  • Right to Anonymization, Blocking, or Deletion: Request anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary or illegally processed personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Deletion of Data Processed with Consent: Request deletion of personal data processed based on your consent, except when retention is required by law.
  • Right to Information About Data Sharing: Ask for information about public or private entities with which we share your personal data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing activities at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with Brazil’s National Data Protection Authority (ANPD) if you believe your data rights have been violated.

To exercise your LGPD rights, contact us at dev.appbelieve@gmail.com with the subject line “LGPD Data Request.” We will respond in accordance with Brazil’s data protection law.
